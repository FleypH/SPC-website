Invest With Us

Investing in Africa's Next Financial Infrastructure

Shield Point Capital is not simply building another lending institution.

We are building a scalable financial ecosystem designed to connect borrowers, financial institutions and future financial services through one technology platform.

Our long-term vision extends beyond lending. We are creating infrastructure capable of supporting multiple financial products under a single trusted ecosystem.

Why Invest In Shield Point Capital?

01

Marketplace Before Market Share

Traditional lenders compete for customers.

Shield Point Capital is building the marketplace where customers and financial institutions meet.

Through FinEra Inclusive Credit Marketplace, licensed financial institutions gain access to qualified borrowers, while borrowers receive transparent access to multiple financing options through one platform.

As marketplace adoption grows, every new participant strengthens the ecosystem for everyone.

02

We Participate in the Marketplace We Own

Unlike traditional marketplace operators, Shield Point Capital will also provide selected financial products within the ecosystem.

This creates two complementary revenue opportunities:

  • Revenue generated from our own financial products.
  • Revenue generated from referrals, commissions or platform subscriptions from participating financial institutions.

Owning both the platform and participating within it creates diversified and scalable income streams.

03

A Scalable Business Model

Our growth is not dependent on opening additional physical branches.

Technology enables us to expand efficiently across cities, industries and eventually international markets with significantly lower operational costs than traditional financial institutions.

Each additional lender, borrower and financial product increases the overall value of the platform while improving operating efficiency.

04

Multiple Growth Opportunities

As adoption increases, Shield Point Capital intends to expand into additional financial services, including:

  • Salary-Based Loans
  • Collateral-Based Business Loans
  • Students Portal based loans
  • Agricultural Finance
  • Supply Chain Finance
  • Insurance Distribution
  • Investment Products
  • Savings Solutions
  • Digital Payments
  • Cross-border Financial Services

This diversified strategy creates opportunities for sustainable long-term growth while reducing dependence on any single product.

05

Built Around Financial Inclusion

Millions of Africans remain underserved by traditional financial systems.

Rather than replacing financial institutions, Shield Point Capital enables them to reach more qualified customers through a transparent digital marketplace that improves access to responsible finance.

Long-Term Vision

Our ambition is to become one of Africa's leading financial technology ecosystems.

We envision a future where individuals, businesses and financial institutions interact through one trusted platform that simplifies borrowing, investing, payments and other financial services.

As the ecosystem expands, Shield Point Capital evolves from a lending company into a financial infrastructure business serving multiple sectors across Africa.
