Partner With Us

Partner With Africa's Financial Infrastructure

Shield Point Capital partners with financial institutions, employers, universities, and development organizations to expand access to responsible finance across Africa.

Through the FinEra Inclusive Credit Marketplace and our core services platform, we help partners reach qualified customers, digitize financial products, and grow within a transparent, technology-driven ecosystem.

Whether you are integrating lending rails, co-distributing products, or building inclusion programmes, we provide the infrastructure and local expertise to move from consultation to launch efficiently.

How Partnership Works

01

Consultation

Initial discovery call to understand your goals, market, and integration requirements.

02

Needs Assessment

Our team maps your use case to the right products, APIs, and compliance framework.

03

Integration

Technical onboarding, sandbox testing, and go-live support with dedicated engineers.

04

Launch

Coordinated rollout with marketing support, monitoring, and ongoing partnership management.
