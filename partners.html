Who We Partner With

We work with a diverse range of organizations committed to financial inclusion. Whether you are a bank, employer, or development agency, we have a partnership model designed for your goals.

Banks & MFIs

Extend lending capacity, co-lend on our platform, and reach underserved segments with digital-first products.

Universities

Provide student financing, bursary disbursement, and campus payment solutions through integrated APIs.

Employers

Offer salary-linked lending, payroll integration, and employee financial wellness programmes at scale.

SACCOs

Digitize member services, enable mobile savings, and expand credit access for cooperative members.

Dev Organizations

Deploy capital for financial inclusion programmes with transparent reporting and impact measurement.

Gov Institutions

Support social disbursement, SME stimulus programmes, and regulatory-compliant fintech infrastructure.
